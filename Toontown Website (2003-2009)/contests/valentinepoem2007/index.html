A big thank you to all the Toons who entered the Toontown Valentine's Poem Contest. Here are the five winning entries. Each wins a Disney Pix Max 3MP Digital Camera! Congratulations!


Kate F., 8

"Every day is like Valentine's Day in Toontown"

It was Valentine's Day when I made my Toon,
I wanted to join Flippy and my friends real soon.
Shirts and pants were chosen with shades of red,
In order to match my heart shaped bed.
Daisy Gardens is a good place to start,
Because every Toon has a big heart.
Valentine's Day may come once a year,
But in Toontown its spirit is always near.


Molly B., 44

Roses are red, my children are blue.
They never get to play Toontown, because Mom’s playing too!
My Toon has more Laff points, and the Gags are much stronger.
My kids ask to play, and I say "Just a little bit longer."
My kids' Toons would be stronger, if they ever got to play.
But Mom's busy playing Toontown all night and all day!
There are Cog buildings to rescue, and C.F.O.'s to defeat.
"Kids, it'll be your turn, in just one more week."
I think I have the answer, a perfect prescription.
My kids will get to play, if I buy another subscription!


Sammie Jo R., 8

The Cogs are searching high and low,
Where can the heart of Toontown be?
Perhaps it's hiding on a street,
Or growing in a playground tree.
To find and break it is their task,
But broken it can never be.
For Toontown has so many hearts,
And each one's in a Toon like me!


Lee G., 73

"With apologies to William Shakespeare and Sonnet XVIII."

Shall I compare thee to a Cog-free day?
Thou art more Toonful and more temperate.
Sellbots may vex our daring Toons at play,
And V.P. runs seem all too tough, Toons state.
Sometimes too hot the eye of CFO does shine,
And often does he make my Toon go sad.
Thus, Binky craves a trap of Level Nine,
And perfect craners, treasures for each lad.
But thy eternal Laff points will not fade,
Nor lose possession of the Gags thou lovest.
Nor shall Chief Justice lock you in his shade,
When, in the Brrrgh, Head Hunter Bossbot rovest:
So long as children laugh and Bear Acuda play,
So long will Disney's Toontown hold its sway!


Christian S., 8

Roses are red, Cold Callers are blue,
When we're in Toontown there's plenty to do.
We ride on the Trolley, play games with our friends,
Fish and fight Cogs - the fun never ends.
I love playing Toontown from morn until eve,
If you were in Toontown then I'd never leave.
We'd play with our Doodles and fight the V.P.,
'Till we drift off to Dreamland and share one big ZZZ.
