Check out the winners of our first-ever Halloween Poetry Contest. These poems were super Toontastic and ultra spooky, including Grand Prize Winner Caitlyn B., 12, and First Prize Winners Candice H., 28, Daniella W., 11, Lizzy V., 13, and Nicole E., 11.

Caitlyn B., 12
Tossing and turning all through the night,
October can give us all quite a good fright.
On Halloween night if you let your guard down,
Nothing can save you in all of Toontown.
The ghouls will get you, no more you'll be seen,
On this dreadful night we call Halloween.
Won't matter at all if you're a black cat,
Now take this in heed, and I'll leave it at that!

Candice H., 28
"Boo! Did I scare you?" is the theme of the day,
Black cats are out Trick or Treating, Jack O' Lanterns light the way.
Beware of the Bloodsuckers out looking for a bite,
But they are no match for the Toons out tonight.
Toons are dressed up in their Halloween clothes,
They look "Spooktastic!" from their pumpkin heads to their toes.
It all makes for a haunting good time, I can't lie,
The only thing missing is the pumpkin pie!

Daniella V., 11
Attention all Toons, Halloween's almost here,
Go out to fight Cogs, don't tremble in fear.
If you don't like to battle, there's much more than that,
Order from Clarabelle's Cattlelog or make a black cat!
Buy pumpkins and shirts, wallpaper too,
On Halloween in Toontown, there's so much to do!
So go on your computer and make a new Toon,
We'll take a ride on the trolley, hope to see you soon!

Lizzy V., 13
Long ago, on a Halloween night,
Bloodsuckers filled Toontown with a fright.
All except for one poor little Cog,
Who shook and shivered in scary fog.
No matter how hard he tried to look grim,
Toons always ended up scaring him!
The Toons all laughed as he ran away,
But he grew up to be frightening, to this very day!

Nicole E., 11
Alto Avenue's the best street, with 36 shops to Trick-or-Treat
The Hard Rock Shop can be forgotten, the candy there is really rotten
Gardening can't get you any candy, but you can grow a Marzi Pansy
If chocolate's what you're looking for, at Moonbeam's Ice Creams try the door
Professor Pete, who "restocks all," gives every treat, both big and small
When a pumpkin head's what you desire, visit Lyre Lyre Lyre Pants on Fire
Bloodsucker Cogs are really mean, they think they're special on Halloween
But do you know Toontown's best trick? When eating treats, you won't get sick!
