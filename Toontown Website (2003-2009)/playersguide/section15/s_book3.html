When you start training for a new gag track, this page comes to life! Check it out as you complete your gag training ToonTasks to see how close you are to using your new gags!


The Cog Gallery page in your Shticker book helps you keep track of how many of each type of Cog you’ve defeated. Once you fill your quota and defeat all the Cogs listed, you get a reward - Cog Radar! Click on the Bossbot, Lawbot, Cashbot, or Sellbot logo, and the number of Cogs on the street you’re on will be revealed.
After Cog Radar is earned, a new quota will appear in the Cog Gallery. Once the second quota is filled, the ability to see what kinds of buildings are on each street becomes available - Building Radar.



Check the fishing page to see what varieties of fish you’ve caught, the fish you have in your bucket, and what type of fishing rod you have. You can also view all your fishing trophies here.

Toon Tip: For every 10 new species of fish you catch you’ll earn a fishing trophy - and a one-point Laff boost!
