Be sure to keep your Shticker Book with you at all times - this is your guide to Toontown! Your Shticker Book holds your Toontown map, keeps track of your gags and jellybeans, and helps you keep track of your ToonTasks. It’s also where you’ll find your Cog Radar. Open your Shticker Book any time by clicking the icon in the lower right corner of the screen.

 Toon Tip: Press the "F8" button on your keyboard to open Shticker Book more quickly.
All of Toontown’s system stuff is tucked away here. Use this page to turn music and sound effects on and off and change your display settings - you can use this page to have Toontown display inside a window if you like. You can also decide if you want to accept requests for new Toontown friends and change the color of your SpeedChat menu from here.



Every district in the Tooniverse is displayed here with the number of Toons currently playing in each. To switch districts, just double-click on the name.
For advanced Toons only: Stars earned defeating Cog buildings are specific to Toontown’s districts. If you leave a district, you’ll leave your stars behind!
