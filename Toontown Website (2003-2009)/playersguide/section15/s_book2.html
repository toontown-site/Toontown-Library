When you first start playing, the map will be covered with clouds! As you explore the streets of Toontown the different neighborhoods will be revealed to you. Use the map to teleport to your estate or any of Toontown’s neighborhoods. Remember, you’ll need to complete a few ToonTasks to earn the ability to teleport to some locations!

Everything you need to know about your Toon’s gags! See all the gags you’ve earned and how many skill points you need to earn your next gag. Keep track of how many gags you are carrying and check how many jellybeans are in your jar from this page. Refer to Chapter Three of the Player’s Guide for more information.
Toon Tip: Press the "home" button on your keyboard to view your gags without opening your Shticker Book.

As you learned in Chapter Six, ToonTasks are the quests you are sent on to help save Toontown from the Cogs. They are also the key to becoming a more powerful Toon! Check this page of your Shticker Book to keep track of your current ToonTasks. Roll over any task to see the reward you’ll earn for completing it.
Toon Tip: Press the "end" button on your keyboard to view your ToonTasks without opening your Shticker Book.
