Toontown is the multi player online game that lets you live the life of a Toon. Create your own Toon character, furnish your estate, play games with friends, and explore amazing lands. The best part is it’s always growing, always changing, and as wild as your imagination!

Did we mention the Cogs? These evil robot business types have invaded, and it’s up to you and your friends to join forces to save Toontown! Cogs can’t take a joke, so use your giant arsenal of gags to crack them up. Get out your seltzer bottles, drop a few safes, and let the cream pies fly!
Now that you’ve created your own Toon and completed the Toontorial, you’re ready to earn some jellybeans. Start by getting on the Trolley in Toontown Central and playing some games to earn jellybeans. Jellybeans are the only currency accepted at Goofy’s Gag Shop, and you’ll need to buy gags to defeat those humorless Cogs. Once you have a gag-filled arsenal, you are ready to head over to Punchline Place, Loopy Lane, or Silly Street to battle the Cogs in the streets. They won’t be hard to find -- they’re everywhere!

When you’re ready for a real challenge, team up with a few other Toons and head for a Cog building. On the highest floors you’ll encounter Loan Sharks, Minglers, and other truly tedious Cogs - tough robots to get a good laugh out of.
Every Toon starts the game with the basics: 15 Laff points and the ability to carry up to 20 gags - ten cupcake Throw gags and ten squirting flower Squirt gags. You can also work on one ToonTask at a time. As you play Toontown and complete your tasks and earn more Laff points, you’ll find your Toon becomes stronger, can carry more gags, and can work on multiple tasks at the same time.
