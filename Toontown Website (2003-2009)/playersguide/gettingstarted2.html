You’ll earn skill points for every gag you use in the battle, increasing your abilities with your gags. When you’ve earned enough skill points with a certain type of gag, you’ll unlock a new, more powerful gag you can use to take on bigger, nastier Cogs.
Everyone needs friends, and Toons are no exception! As your Toon progresses, he/she will have to take over Cog buildings to complete a lot of ToonTasks. Make sure you have lots of friends who can help you out, because most Cog buildings are too tough for a single Toon to handle alone. Battling Cogs together is also a lot of laffs!

Remember, it takes teamwork to battle the Cogs, so just like you need your friends to help complete your ToonTasks, your friends need you!

As any Toon will tell you, moving your Toon around Toontown is simple.
Use the arrow keys to move forward and backward, and turn right and left. The control key lets you jump.

You can look up using the page up key to look up, and the page down key to look down.
The tab key lets you change the angle of your camera, letting you get different views of your Toon.

Use the end key any time you want to take a quick peek at your ToonTasks.

The F7 key pops up your Friends panel.

The F8 key opens your Shticker book.

The F9 key takes a screenshot that is stored in your Toontown folder on your computer.
