There are six neighborhoods in Toontown: Toontown Central, Donald’s Dock, Daisy Gardens, Minnie’s Melodyland, The Brrrgh, and Donald’s Dreamland. Official Toontown citizens can go anywhere they want any time they want, but most ToonTasks will keep you in one of the neighborhoods.

Secret Tip: If you haven’t yet earned teleport access to a neighborhood you want to visit, check your friends list and see if anyone you know is there. If they are, use the "go to" button and teleport to them. We call it TRT - Toon Rapid Transit!

The busting hub of Toontown is home to Mickey’s Toon Hall, the School House, and the Library. Here you can fish in the peaceful quiet of a pond, while away the hours chatting with friends in the gazebo, or simply stretch your legs on the rolling greens, admiring the statues of some of Toontown’s greatest heroes.
