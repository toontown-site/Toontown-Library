On the hazy shores east of Toontown Central lies the seaside neighborhood of Donald’s Dock. If you have a chance, take a swim in the playground and search for starfish. They can be scarce, but rumor has it they’ll help fill your Laff meter if you can find them. Donald will be happy to give you a ride on his boat - but be warned he’s sticking close to the playground, keeping a weather eye out for Cogs!
