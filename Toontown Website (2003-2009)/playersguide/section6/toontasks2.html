To complete your training, you’ll need to finish a set of gag training ToonTasks. Each time you complete a gag training task, one of the spots on the Gag Track Training page of your Shticker Book is filled in. When you’ve completed all fifteen tasks, you’ll need to finish just one last ToonTask to earn your new gag.
These ToonTasks give you the ability to jump to one of Toontown’s playgrounds using the map in the Shticker Book. Teleport tasks are very rare, and can be very helpful.
A bigger gag pouch means you can carry more gags. As you earn new gags and start to take over bigger, tougher Cog buildings, you’ll discover you need to carry a lot of gags.
The more jellybeans you can carry, the easier it is to stock up on gags fast. A big jellybean jar will really come in handy when you are taking over lots of Cog buildings with other Toons - you don’t want to keep them waiting!
