ToonTasks are the quests you are sent on to help save Toontown from the Cogs. They are also the key to becoming a more powerful Toon! For each task you complete, your Toon will be rewarded with Laffboosts, new gags, jellybeans, and more. If you have any open task slots, visit Toon Headquarters or any of ToonTown’s shops and get a new ToonTask quick!
Laffboosts increase the amount of Laff points your Laff meter can hold. This is also called your Laff limit. You will need to keep increasing your Laff limit as you advance through the game because the more Laff points your Laff meter can hold, the harder it is for Cogs to make you sad. Attacks by bigger Cogs you’ll encounter later in the game can pack a real punch and take the Laff right out of you! When you see a LaffBoost task, choose it to increase your Laff limit.

Ready to add new gags to your pouch? Then start your gag track training. Every Toon starts with Squirt and Throw, but from there the choice is yours. Think carefully before starting to train a new type of gag: each one has its strengths and weaknesses. It’s a good idea to find some Toons that already have the gags you’re thinking about training, and see how they use them. This might help you decide if it’s right for you.
