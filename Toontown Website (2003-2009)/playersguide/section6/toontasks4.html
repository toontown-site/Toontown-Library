If any of the task spots in your Shticker Book are blank, it’s time to get a new ToonTask. Just walk up to an officer in any Toon HQ building, or visit one of the shops in Toontown’s streets.

Toon Tip: If you don’t like the tasks you see at first, try talking to a different HQ Officers, or try visiting a different shop or HQ building. You might find something you like better!

Helping other Toons helps you too. Helping a friend finish one of their ToonTasks is a good way to make sure your friends will be there to help you with your toughest ToonTasks when you need them the most!
Your progress in the game doesn’t stop when you’re helping another Toon with their tasks. Remember that even if the Cogs you’re battling don’t count for your ToonTasks, you’re still earning gag skill points and filling your Cog Gallery. Most importantly, you’re helping a friend in need!
When you see one of your ToonTasks is complete, just follow the instructions at the bottom to turn it in and get your reward and restock your Laff meter. Don’t forget to get a new task while you’re at it!
