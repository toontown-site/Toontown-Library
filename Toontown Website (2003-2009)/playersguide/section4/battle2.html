Using gags in battle earns you skill points. When you’ve earned enough skill points you will get access to bigger, more powerful gags.

Your skill credit is multiplied in some battles. If you are in a building the skill credit you earn increases as you move up the floors. During an invasion you get double skill credit. If you are in a building during an invasion you get double credit for the building!


Example:
If you use a Whole Cream Pie, normally worth five skill points, on the third floor of a building, you will receive 10 skill points. If there is also an invasion happening, you will get 20 skill points!
Warning!   You won’t get any points for using a big gag on a little Cog. You can see if you will earn skill points by looking at the information on the right side of the battle menu display when you roll over any of your gags. Gags that won't get you experience also have a green background instead of the normal blue.
In the Cog HQ Senior V.P. battle, during the first round against Cogs you get double skill credit, during the second round against Skelecogs you get triple skill credit!
You should always carry as many ToonTasks as you can, and always train for a new gag track if you can. Make every battle count!
