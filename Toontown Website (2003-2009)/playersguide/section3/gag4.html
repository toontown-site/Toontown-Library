Lure gags bring the Cogs closer to you. Why the heck would you want to do that?

  • Lured Cogs won’t attack unless they are woken up by a squirt, throw or sound gag.
  • Squirt and throw gags pack a bigger punch against lured Cogs - 20% more power!
  • You’ll have to lure a Cog to get it to fall for a Trap gag.
Like the Toon-up gags, some Lure gags only affect one Cog, while others affect all Cogs.
 
 
Toon Tip: Lured Cogs won’t stay lured forever, even if they aren’t attacked. Eventually, they will come back to life and attack you!
 
Sound gags hit every Cog at once, but are not very powerful. Sound gags are even more accurate than Squirt gags; they rarely miss.
 
 
Toon Tip: Think twice before using a Sound gag on lured Cogs. It will wake them from their lured state!
