Toon Tip: If you type a period in the chat window before typing anything else, the message will appear as a thought bubble instead of a speech bubble and will stay on screen longer.
SpeedChat is a set of ready-made phrases you can use to talk to any Toon you like. It’s a great way to talk about your ToonTasks, plan activities, strategize during a battle, and best of all, make friends.
Just click on the green SpeedChat button at the top left corner of the screen, and choose any phrase you want. Every Toon in the area will hear what you have to say.

Toon Tip: The SpeedChat phrases in the ToonTasks menu will change depending on what tasks you are working on. This is an easy way to let your friends know what you’re up to.
If you want to talk to just one Toon, click on them! A small panel will appear with both the blue chat button and the green SpeedChat button. Click the green button to whisper to that Toon using the SpeedChat phrases. No other Toon will be able to see these private messages.

Toon Tip: You can reply to another Toon’s whisper by clicking on one of their whisper message bubbles. Try doing this when you can’t see the Toon you want to respond to on screen.
