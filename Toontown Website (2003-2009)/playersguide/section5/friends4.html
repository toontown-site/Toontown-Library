Asking another Toon to help you take over a Cog building - You never know what another Toon might be up to, so make sure you’re polite when you ask for help.


Accepting an invitation to take over a Cog building - Make sure you are loaded up on gags and Laff points before you help take over a Cog building. If you need to visit a gag shop or get some secret items in a playground, let them know. Your fellow Toons will be happy to wait a minute while you prepare for battle.


Telling another Toon you’ll join them as soon as you can - Sometimes an invitation to join a friend comes when you’re doing something else. You might be in a Cog building, in a battle on a street, finishing a delivery task, fishing, or playing a Trolley game.


Enlisting other Toons - If it’s just you and another Toon in front of a Cog building, you should probably find some friends to help. If you want to call your friends, let the other Toon know.
