Battle strategy - Some phrases can be used when you need to help a less experienced Toon get through a tricky battle.
You can use phrases like these to tell a Toon you like the way they are playing.
Declining an invitation to join another Toon - If you don’t think you can come anytime soon (for example, you’re on the third floor of a four story building) you might just want to say no. You don’t want to keep your friends waiting too long.
Telling other Toons what you're working on - Your SpeedChat phrases automatically change when you start or finish a ToonTask. When a Toon asks you what you’re working on, just open up the ToonTasks area of your SpeedChat menu and let them know!
